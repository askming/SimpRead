#### Where Does the Poisson Distribution Come From?

The Poisson distribution is named after French mathematician Sim√©on Denis Poisson, who introduced it in the 19th century to model/understand rare events especially in scenarios where it‚Äôs unlikely for them to happen frequently in short bursts. Initially, it was used for scenarios like predicting how many soldiers might be injured by accidents (e.g., horse kicks) in a given time period.
